Soft Tofu Soup

Soft Tofu Soup is a traditional Korean dish, usually made in an earthenware pot. It's great in the wintertime, when you need a nice warm bowl of soup to escape the cold winter weather.

Ingredients List:

  • Soft Tofu
  • Anchovy Stock
  • Scallions
  • Ground Pork
  • Chili Pepper Flakes
  • Sesame Oil
  • Chili Pepper Paste

Directions:

  1. Create scallion oil by frying your chopped scallions in oil.
  2. Add in your ground pork and other vegetables you might want to add
  3. Meanwhile, start heating up your anchovy stock in the earthenware bowl.
  4. While the stock is heating up, add Chili Pepper Flakes and Chili Pepper paste into the pan.
  5. Once the stock is properly heated, add the contents of the pan into the earthenware bowl.
  6. Mix around the ingredients, and then add your soft tofu into the bowl.
  7. With a spoon, cut up the soft tofu into big pieces
  8. Lastly, add in an egg for extra flavor.
  9. Enjoy your Soft Tofu Soup!
